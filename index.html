Reality vs Virtual

在虚拟世界中徘徊，键盘声伴随着心跳，指尖舞动在数字间，身体在网络中飘摇。


眼睛沉溺于光影，思绪在代码中游荡，身心如网线交织，虚实交融在时空中。


数字花园绽放光华，身体投射在幻影中，网络的迷雾笼罩，身在其中，心难自由。

时光匆匆如梭，身体被网络所拘，虚拟世界的幻境，魅力无法抵挡的诱惑。

但忘记身体的根基，终将迷失在虚空，唯有珍惜眼前，才能解脱网络的纠缠。
Wandering around the virtual world, The sound of the keyboard accompanied the heartbeat, Moving your fingers through the numbers, The body is floating in the network.
Eyes addicted to light and shadow, Thoughts wander through the code, The body and mind are intertwined like a network cable, The virtual and the real blend in time and space.
Digital garden in full bloom, The body is projected in a phantom, The fog of the Internet, It's hard to be free in it.
Time flies, The body is trapped by the Internet, A fantasy of the virtual world, The irresistible lure of charm.
But forget the foundation of the body, Eventually lost in the void, Only cherish the present, To untangle the network.
